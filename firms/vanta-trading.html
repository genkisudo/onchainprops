Vanta Trading

Onchain prop firm built on Bittensor Subnet 8, with up to $100K funded accounts and an optional Hyperliquid-native mode.

Split: 100% Max Account: $100,000 Chain: Bittensor / Hyperliquid Token: No

Vanta Trading is a prop firm built by Taoshi, the team behind Bittensor Subnet 8. Trading occurs in a simulated environment that mirrors real market prices — orders do not touch live order books by default. A separate Hyperliquid-native mode is available for eligible traders. Funded accounts come with a flat 100% profit split. Payouts are processed in USD via Stripe, not stablecoins.

Account Sizes and Scaling

$100,000 is the highest starting tier (Tier IV) — it is not the ceiling. Funded accounts can scale up to $2,500,000 through Vanta's scaling program.

Profit Targets

Profit targets vary by market: 8% for Forex, and 10% for Crypto, Commodities, and Equities.

Business Model

Vanta generates revenue from fees, but that's not the core of the business. Long-term value comes from successful traders improving the network itself: the better traders perform, the stronger the signal quality, capital allocation, and overall network performance become.

Glitch

The network performance thesis is central to Glitch, a SaaS platform built on top of Vanta that lets investors and the team copy trade the best traders on the network.
