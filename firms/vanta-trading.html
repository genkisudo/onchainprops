Vanta Trading

Onchain prop firm on Hyperliquid with up to $100K funded accounts.

Split: Up to 100% Max Account: $100,000 Chain: Hyperliquid Token: No

Vanta Trading is an onchain prop firm operating on Hyperliquid with up to a 100% profit split and accounts up to $100,000. Vanta leverages Hyperliquid's high-performance trading environment using Hyperscaled infrastructure, which mirrors trader activity in the background — combining decentralization with a familiar, performance-focused trading environment to help traders operate at their highest level.

Business Model

Vanta generates revenue from fees, but that's not the core of the business. Long-term value comes from successful traders improving the network itself: the better traders perform, the stronger the signal quality, capital allocation, and overall network performance become.

Vanta is A-booking — trader profits are paid from platform revenue, not offset against the firm's own positions.

Glitch

The network performance thesis is central to Glitch, a SaaS platform built on top of Vanta that lets investors and the team copy trade the best traders on the network.
