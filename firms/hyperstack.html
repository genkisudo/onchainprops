HyperStack

Scaled prop firm capital built for Hyperliquid traders. Trade through your own Hyperliquid wallet, pass a one-step challenge, and qualify for scaled accounts up to $400K.

Split: Up to 90% Max Account: $400,000 scaled account Chain: Hyperliquid Token: No Country: BVI

What Is HyperStack?

HyperStack is a Hyperliquid-native decentralized prop trading firm. Traders connect their Hyperliquid wallet, trade as they normally would, and HyperStack mirrors eligible trading performance into a scaled account.

Unlike traditional prop firms, HyperStack is non-custodial: there are no API keys, no custody transfer, and no proprietary trading platform. Rules are published upfront, enforced programmatically, and rewards for eligible participants are paid in USDC onchain.

Jurisdiction: HyperStack is registered in the British Virgin Islands (BVI).

HyperStack Profit Split

HyperStack uses a 90/10 rewards structure for scaled traders. Traders keep 90% of performance-based rewards, all paid in USDC.

Rewards are based on consistent trading performance. Passing the challenge does not guarantee a payout or compensation.

HyperStack Account Types

HyperStack offers one-step challenge accounts:

  • $5K account
  • $10K account
  • $25K account
  • $50K account
  • $100K account

All paid accounts can scale up to a maximum account size of $400K through continued performance.

Trading Conditions

Profit target: 10% during the challenge.

Max drawdown: 5% during the challenge.

Time limit: None, although 30 days of inactivity results in elimination.

Trading: news trading, weekend trading, and algorithmic (agent and bot) trading are allowed.

Payouts: eligible scaled participants receive monthly USDC rewards directly to their wallet, verifiable onchain.

What Makes HyperStack Different?

HyperStack is built specifically for Hyperliquid traders. Users trade from their own wallet on Hyperliquid while HyperStack tracks performance in the background. The platform also includes an open-source Chrome extension that shows live progress, drawdown limits, mirrored position sizing, and rule guardrails directly inside Hyperliquid.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does HyperStack require API keys or custody of my funds?

No. HyperStack is non-custodial. You trade from your own Hyperliquid wallet — no API keys, no custody transfer, and no proprietary platform required. HyperStack mirrors your trading performance in the background.

What is the profit target and drawdown limit for the HyperStack challenge?

The one-step challenge requires a 10% profit target with a 5% max drawdown limit. There is no time limit, but 30 days of inactivity results in elimination.

How do HyperStack payouts work?

Eligible scaled participants receive monthly USDC rewards paid directly to their Hyperliquid wallet, verifiable onchain. Passing the challenge does not guarantee a payout — rewards are based on consistent trading performance.

What account sizes does HyperStack offer?

HyperStack offers paid accounts at $5K, $10K, $25K, $50K, and $100K. All accounts can scale up to a maximum of $400K through continued performance.
