Onchain crypto prop trading firm on Solana offering up to $100K in funded accounts and up to a 90% profit split.

About Solana Funded — How the Prop Firm Works

What is Solana Funded and how does it work?
Solana Funded is an onchain proprietary trading firm built on the Solana blockchain. Traders pass an evaluation phase to demonstrate disciplined risk management, then receive a funded account with up to $100,000 in capital. You trade with the firm's capital and keep up to 90% of the profits you generate.

What is a crypto prop firm?
A prop firm (proprietary trading firm) is a company that lets traders use the firm's capital instead of risking their own money. Solana Funded is a crypto-native prop firm built on the Solana blockchain, taking advantage of Solana's speed and low transaction costs for an optimal trading experience.

Why trade with Solana Funded instead of using your own capital?
When you trade on a standard exchange, you risk your own savings — a losing trade is your money gone. With Solana Funded, you pay an evaluation fee to join, then trade with the firm's capital. Your risk is capped at the evaluation fee, and you keep up to 90% of the profits when you trade successfully.

Why Solana for Prop Trading?

Why trade on Solana instead of other chains?
Solana offers sub-second transaction finality, extremely low fees (fractions of a cent per transaction), and a growing DeFi ecosystem with perpetual DEXs. This makes it ideal for active trading where speed and cost efficiency matter. Unlike EVM chains where gas fees can eat into profits during volatile markets, Solana keeps execution costs negligible.

What markets can I trade on Solana Funded?
Solana Funded focuses on crypto perpetual futures trading on the Solana blockchain. The Solana ecosystem supports a range of perpetual DEXs with deep liquidity across major crypto pairs.

Solana Funded Profit Split and Payouts

What profit split does Solana Funded offer?
Solana Funded offers up to a 90% profit split, meaning traders keep up to 90% of the profits they generate from funded accounts. This is among the highest splits available in the onchain prop trading space.

What is the maximum account size?
The maximum funded account size on Solana Funded is $100,000. This allows traders to scale their strategies with significant capital backing.

How do payouts work on Solana Funded?
Payouts are processed directly to your non-custodial wallet, typically in stablecoins like USDC on the Solana network. This provides fast, transparent settlement without traditional bank delays or arbitrary holds.

Getting Started with Solana Funded

How do I start trading with Solana Funded?
Visit the Solana Funded website, choose an evaluation tier, and complete the evaluation by hitting profit targets while staying within drawdown limits. Once you pass, you receive access to a funded account and can begin trading with the firm's capital.

Do I need trading experience?
While prior trading experience is helpful, the key requirement is disciplined risk management. Follow the evaluation rules — mainly drawdown limits — and demonstrate consistent trading to qualify for funding.
