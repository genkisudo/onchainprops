ProprXYZ Review

Transparent onchain prop trading firm on Hyperliquid with up to $200K funded accounts, 80% profit split, and 150+ tradable assets.

What Is ProprXYZ?

ProprXYZ is a transparent onchain prop trading firm built on Hyperliquid, a decentralized perpetual futures exchange. Unlike traditional prop firms, Propr publicly shares everything competitors hide: pass rates, revenue numbers, payout transactions, and the A/B booking algorithm.

Propr offers a wide investment universe with perpetual futures across crypto, equities, and commodities, covering 150+ assets all in one terminal.

ProprXYZ Profit Split

Funded traders keep 80% of all profits. The remaining 20% goes to Propr. The profit split applies to all funded accounts regardless of account size or evaluation type (1-Step or 2-Step). There are no tiered splits or conditions attached.

ProprXYZ Payout Structure

Each payout sweeps all available profit. There are no partial withdrawals. You must close all open positions before requesting a payout. After payout, your account balance resets to the initial funded amount. There is no maximum payout amount and no cap on how many times you can request payouts. No payout fees.

Risk limits: Max daily loss and max drawdown apply at all times on both evaluation and funded accounts.

ProprXYZ Fee Structure

ProprXYZ replicates Hyperliquid's native fee schedule with no additional markups or hidden charges:

Equities, commodities, and FX perpetuals: Fees mirror the fee structure set by the underlying HIP-3 deployer on Hyperliquid. Each asset's fee schedule follows its respective deployer configuration.

ProprXYZ Maximum Daily Loss Rules

The maximum daily loss caps how much your equity can drop within a single day. The dollar limit is fixed at a percentage of your challenge starting balance and does not scale with account growth.

1-Step accounts: 3% of starting balance. 2-Step accounts: 5% of starting balance. Reset time: 00:00 UTC daily.

The daily loss limit is set once at account creation using the challenge starting balance and never changes: Daily Loss Limit = Starting Balance × Daily Loss %. Each day at 00:00 UTC, the system snapshots your equity. Your equity floor for the next 24 hours is that snapshot minus the fixed daily loss limit.

ProprXYZ Maximum Drawdown Rules

The maximum drawdown sets an absolute floor for the account: the lowest your equity can ever go. The calculation differs between 1-Step and 2-Step accounts.

1-Step accounts (static drawdown): Max Drawdown Equity Limit = Starting Balance × (1 − 0.06). This limit is set once at account creation and never changes, regardless of how high the account grows.

Example: you have a $50,000 1-Step account. The max drawdown equity limit is $47,000. You trade profitably and grow the account to $60,000. Your max drawdown limit is still $47,000 — it does not trail upward. If your equity drops to $47,000 at any point, even intraday on an open position, the account is breached.

Onchain Trade Verification and Transparency (coming soon)

All A-booked trades executed on ProprXYZ accounts are routed through Hyperliquid and are verifiable onchain. This means:

Every A-booked trade, including entries, exits, and liquidations, is recorded on Hyperliquid's blockchain. Traders can independently verify A-booked trade history onchain. Propr's aggregate payout history will be published onchain.

B-booked trades (internal book entries) are not routed to Hyperliquid and are therefore not visible onchain. The P&L outcome is identical regardless of execution method. Every trade on your Propr dashboard is labeled as A-booked or B-booked, so you always have full visibility into how each trade was executed.
